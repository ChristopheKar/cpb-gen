Welcome to the CPB Image Generation and Pest Detection Tool!

These applications were developed by the Humans and Machines Lab at the American University of Beirut under the WEFRAH initiative, tackling plant health.

The Generation app can be used to label objects within images using bounding boxes, and even artificially augment these images with new objects to reduce data collection and labeling efforts.

The Detection app can be used to detect Bemisia tabaci whiteflies on plant leaves.
